Have a question? Check out the FAQ

We're here to help! Check out some common questions and answers below:

What is included in the Basic Plan?

The Basic Plan includes access to core course materials, video lessons, and a certificate upon completion.

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply create an account, choose your course, and follow the checkout process to enroll.

Can I access the course materials offline?

Yes, selected materials can be downloaded for offline study, depending on the course.

Is there a certificate after completing the course?

Yes, you’ll receive a digital certificate once you successfully complete all requirements.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment methods.

Can I get a refund if I’m not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a refund within 7 days of purchase, as long as the course hasn’t been fully completed.
